About Me

Passionate about movement and expression

With over a decade of professional dance experience, I've dedicated my life to the art of movement and storytelling through dance. My journey began at the Royal Academy of Dance, where I received classical training in ballet before expanding my repertoire to include contemporary, jazz, and modern dance styles.

I've performed with prestigious dance companies across North America and Europe, including the New York Contemporary Dance Theater and London Modern Ballet. My choreography has been featured in numerous productions, music videos, and international dance festivals.

As an instructor and mentor, I'm passionate about helping dancers of all levels discover their unique voice and push the boundaries of their abilities. Whether I'm performing on stage or teaching in the studio, my goal is to inspire and create meaningful connections through the universal language of dance.
